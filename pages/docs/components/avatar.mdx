## AvatarGroup

The `AvatarGroup` component is used to display a group of avatars.

<AvatarGroupExample size={40} />

### with `max` property

The `max` property limits the number of avatars displayed. The remaining avatars are represented by a `+{number}` avatar.
Also in this example `surplusClassName` property was useed to add custom styles to the `+{number}` avatar.

<AvatarGroupExample max={3} surplusClassName='!bg-error-200' />

### with `total` property

The `total` property is used to display the total number
of avatars. The remaining avatars are represented by a `+{number}` avatar.

<AvatarGroupExample total={325} />
